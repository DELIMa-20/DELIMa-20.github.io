Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Google SERP Checker? +

A Google SERP Checker lets you view search results as they appear for users in a specific country. Since Google personalises results based on your IP, what you see may differ from users elsewhere. Valentin App solves this using Google's official geo-location parameters across 195+ countries.

Do I need a VPN to use Valentin App? +

No. Valentin App uses Google's official gl URL parameter to simulate searches from any country — no VPN, proxy, or browser extension needed.

Are the results live or from a database? +
